Set the sale window (valid_from / valid_to) on an existing offering — launch a
    plan on a date, or stop selling it after one. Operator-only, /confirm-gated.

    ELICIT BEFORE YOU CALL. A window is what makes a plan appear in and vanish
    from ``catalog.list_active_offerings``, which is what order creation reads —
    a wrong bound silently unsells a live plan. If the operator has not given at
    least one bound, ask in plain prose and stop.

    Required:
        offering_id: The offering to time-bound.
        at least one of valid_from / valid_to. Passing neither is rejected: it
            would CLEAR both bounds, which is not the same as "leave them alone".

    Bounds:
        valid_from: ISO-8601 — sellable from this instant. Explicit ``null``
            CLEARS the bound (sellable from the beginning).
        valid_to: ISO-8601, EXCLUSIVE — the last instant is just before this.
            Explicit ``null`` CLEARS the bound (no end).

    Both are absolute instants: get them from ``clock.now`` and the operator's
    stated offset. Never fabricate an ISO string, and never turn a vague
    "end of the month" into a date yourself — ask which date they mean.

    Read ``catalog.get_offering(offering_id)`` first and tell the operator the
    CURRENT window in your proposal, so they can see what they are replacing.

    Returns:
        Updated TMF620 offering dict.

    Raises:
        MISSING_REQUIRED_FIELDS: no offering_id, or neither bound supplied.
        PolicyViolationFromServer:
            - ``catalog.offering.not_found``.
